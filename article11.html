Road Rage Incident: Two Arrested for Assault

By Rajesh Kumar | October 12, 2025 | 12:00 PM IST

Two men were arrested for brutally assaulting a cab driver in a road rage incident that was captured on dashcam. The incident occurred on Delhi-Gurgaon expressway when a minor traffic dispute escalated into violence.

The accused, driving a luxury SUV, got angry when the cab driver honked at them for sudden lane change. They forced the cab to stop, pulled out the driver, and beat him with a baseball bat.

The entire incident was recorded on the cab's dashcam. The video went viral on social media, leading to public outrage. Police identified the accused through their vehicle registration and arrested them within hours.

"The video evidence was crucial in this case," said Traffic Police Commissioner. "Such violent behavior on roads will not be tolerated."

The victim suffered multiple injuries and is recovering in hospital. The accused have been charged with attempt to murder, assault, and criminal intimidation. Their vehicle has been impounded and driving licenses suspended.
