Serial Burglar Arrested After 3-Month Chase

By Priya Sharma | October 13, 2025 | 7:00 AM IST

Delhi Police finally nabbed a notorious serial burglar who had been evading capture for three months. The accused, identified as 32-year-old Ramesh Kumar, is suspected of committing over 40 burglaries across South Delhi residential areas.

Kumar was arrested from a hideout in Mehrauli after an intensive surveillance operation. Police recovered stolen jewelry worth Rs 25 lakhs, cash, and electronic items from his possession.

The breakthrough came when police analyzed CCTV footage from multiple crime scenes and identified a pattern in his modus operandi. Kumar would target houses during afternoon hours when residents were away.

"He was a professional burglar who studied his targets carefully before striking," said ACP Crime Branch. "We have been tracking him for months and finally caught him red-handed."

Several victims have identified their stolen items at the police station. Kumar has confessed to his crimes and is being interrogated about possible accomplices.
