Major cybercrime ring busted in Delhi, 15 arrested with fake documents worth crores

Major Cybercrime Ring Busted: 15 Arrested in Delhi Operation

By Rajesh Kumar | October 13, 2025 | 9:30 AM IST

Delhi Police's Cyber Crime Unit conducted a massive raid across multiple locations in the capital, busting a sophisticated cybercrime syndicate. The operation led to the arrest of 15 individuals involved in creating fake documents, identity theft, and online fraud worth over Rs 50 crores.

The accused were operating from rented apartments in South Delhi and had been targeting senior citizens and small business owners through phishing scams. Police recovered laptops, mobile phones, fake Aadhaar cards, and forged bank documents from the premises.
