Bank Heist Foiled: 4 Armed Robbers Arrested

By Priya Sharma | October 12, 2025 | 6:00 PM IST

Quick police action prevented a major bank robbery in Gurgaon. Four armed men were arrested while attempting to rob a nationalized bank branch in Sector 29. Police recovered weapons, masks, and tools used for breaking locks.

The incident occurred when bank security guard noticed suspicious activity outside the bank early morning. He immediately alerted police who reached the spot within minutes.

The robbers, armed with country-made pistols and knives, had planned to strike during opening hours when cash was being loaded into ATMs. They had been surveilling the bank for two weeks.

"Their plan was well-coordinated but our quick response foiled the attempt," said ACP Gurgaon. "No shots were fired and no one was injured."

All four accused have criminal records with previous cases of robbery and assault. They have been remanded to police custody for further interrogation about possible involvement in other crimes.
