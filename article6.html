Businessman Murder: Wife and Lover Arrested

By Rajesh Kumar | October 12, 2025 | 10:00 PM IST

Delhi Police solved a high-profile murder case with the arrest of a businessman's wife and her alleged lover. The victim, 52-year-old Suresh Gupta, was found dead in his Vasant Vihar residence last week.

Initial investigation suggested natural death, but autopsy revealed poisoning. Police then discovered that the victim's wife, Meena Gupta, was having an extramarital affair with family friend Anil Sharma.

The couple had been planning the murder for months. They mixed poison in the victim's evening tea and made it look like a heart attack. However, forensic evidence and phone records exposed their plot.

"They thought they had committed the perfect crime, but modern forensic techniques caught them," said DCP Crime. "Both have confessed to the murder."

The motive was to inherit the victim's property worth Rs 50 crores. Both accused have been charged with murder and conspiracy. The victim's family has expressed shock at the betrayal.
