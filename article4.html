Hit-and-Run Case: Driver Surrenders After CCTV Evidence

By Neha Patel | October 13, 2025 | 4:00 AM IST

A 28-year-old man surrendered to police after CCTV footage clearly showed him fleeing the scene of a fatal hit-and-run accident in Gurgaon. The incident occurred three days ago when his speeding car hit a pedestrian crossing the road.

The victim, a 45-year-old shopkeeper, died on the spot. The driver, identified as Rohit Malhotra, fled immediately without helping the victim or informing authorities.

Police released CCTV footage on social media, which went viral. The clear images of the car and partial view of the driver led to his identification within 24 hours.

"The evidence was overwhelming. He realized he couldn't escape and decided to surrender," said investigating officer Inspector Rajesh Singh.

Malhotra has been charged with culpable homicide not amounting to murder and rash driving. His blood samples have been sent for alcohol testing. The victim's family has demanded strict punishment.
